618-Tinnitus Overview
618-Tinnitus is a Java application that helps with tinnitus relief by playing various sounds that help to mask the noise your brain is creating inside your head. Once the application is running You can choose from natural sounds via the nature button or man made noises via the noise button. You can select up to three different sound to play at the same time. The sounds will play continuously until you click the stop or back button or turn off the application. Your device will need to be running Java 1.8.0 or higher. Click the Free Download button to download 618-Tinnitus. To watch a video tutorial click the red 618-Tube button.

My Story
I suffer with tinnitus and have found the medical advice I have been given to be useless. I have found a sound that masks my tinnitus very well and I play it 24hrs a day in my home. Where I was waking up every morning to a raging sound in my head; I now wake up to my tinnitus relief sound playing quietly near by. I watch television with my relief noise playing beside me. I Hope what I have put together can help someone else find some relief. If you are just now accepting tinnitus as part of your life this is very tough, but it does get easier. Accept it and keep busy.
