618-Calc Overview
618-Calc is a webpage application for solving trigonometry problems. There are three calculators available to choose from;

Phythagoras' Calculator
Use when two sides of a right angle triangle are known and you wish to find the length of the third side.

Trig Calculator (right angle)
Use when finding unknown angles or side lengths for a right angle triangle when two variables are known.

Trig Calculator (cos/sin rule)
Use when finding unknown angles or side lengths for any triangle when three variables are known.
